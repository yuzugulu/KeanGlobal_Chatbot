Office of Student Accounting

Overview
Welcome to the Kean University Office of Student Accounting home page. It is very important to us that each student has a clear understanding when it comes to financing his or her education. This site is designed to provide you with information about the financial aspects of attending Kean, such as tuition and fees, policies and procedures, and various methods of payment. We have anticipated questions and concerns in an effort to minimize any confusion and to make this process as smooth and simple as possible.

Students are billed for tuition, fees, meal plans and housing. We also collect all tuition payments, provide students information on various payment options, apply all loans and financial aid to the student’s accounts, and issue refund checks to students and parents in accordance with state, federal and institutional regulations.

Our Mission
The Office of Student Accounting at Kean University is dedicated to effectively, efficiently and expeditiously provide students, alumni and their families with accurate financial information and guidance with respect to their financial obligations while complying with all policies and guidelines set forth by Kean University and all associated governing bodies. We also aim to provide innovative and comprehensive solutions that allow students convenience and access to technological resources that avail self-service options. Our focus is to keep students at the forefront of our decision making with an emphasis on integrity, courtesy and compassion. The Office of Student Accounting continuously strives for excellence in customer service and adheres to the University mission by engaging in one-on-one financial counseling with students to ensure that they are well advised while navigating campus life.

Kean Counseling Center
How does KCC help?
College can be an exciting and rewarding time in your life. However, it can also be challenging and stressful. These stressors, both large and small, can include:
Social, romantic, or family conflicts
Traumatic incidents
College adjustment struggles
Academic difficulties
Sexual or physical assault                           
Gender identity/sexuality concerns
Drug/alcohol concerns or addiction
In fact, many students may face more than one of these issues over the course of their time at Kean and may struggle to find solutions. These issues can often lead to or exacerbate mental health concerns such as anxiety and depression. Our job at KCC is to assist you to address these obstacles. 

Our goal is to also help you identify your goals. What do you want for yourself? How do you want your life to be? How do you want to relate to others? Our counselors can help you learn more about yourself and others and help you to develop skills and strategies to form stronger and healthier relationships and set and achieve your desired goals. 

Our counselors are experienced licensed professionals who will listen to you without judgment or criticism. Information that you share with us is confidential, meaning no one, including professors, administrators, friends, or family, will be given information about your meetings at the counseling center.
